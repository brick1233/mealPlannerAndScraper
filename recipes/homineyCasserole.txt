Name:
Hominey Casserole

Ingredients:
water - 6 cups
quick cooking grits - 1.5 cups
cheddar cheese - 1 lb
green chile - 4 oz
SALT - 2 tsp
herby salt - 2 teaspoons
eggs - 3

Directions:
Bring water to boil and add grits, cook until thick.
Remove from stove and add remaing ingredients (dice the chiles). Bake in
a greased casserole dish for 1 hour at 350 F. 

Notes:
Substitute for potaotes, to be served with meat.
